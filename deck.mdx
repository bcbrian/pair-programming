# 🍐 Programming
(Opinions Included)

---

## What is 🍐 Programming

 - Often referred to as 🍐ing.
 - It is working with more than one on a task.
 - 🍐s TAKE TURNS!

---

## Why 🍐 Programming

 - Produces better code faster
 - Completes tasks faster 
 - Reduces Silos
 - Creates opportunities to learn
 - Makes you a better person/programer

---

## 🍐 Programming

### It's about Marathons not Sprints

---

## Types of 🍐 Programming

 - 🍐 Programming comes in different flavors and sizes.
 - So do 🍐s.

---

## Structured 🍐ing

 - Structure vs. No Structure.
 - Strive for Structure.
 - Goal or Timed

---

## Attitude of 🍐ing

 - The other person is better than me.
 - Can I show you how I would do that?
 - Help the other to work out their idea before offering yours.

---

## 🍐 Power

 - Strength of 🍐s makes a difference
 - An individual 🍐 might be rotten
 - Sometimes 🍐s are incompatible

---

## Rules for 🍐

 - Understand the task.
 - Define when switching happens.
 - RPS to see who starts. 
 - GO!
 - Adjust as needed.
